About Us


Jockey's Conference Center

Jockey's Conference Center (JCC) is a renowned and prestigious convention center located in the heart of Jakarta, Indonesia. With a rich history spanning several decades, JCC has established itself as a premier venue for hosting a wide range of national and international events.

Our History

JCC first opened its doors in 1974 and has since played a significant role in shaping the event landscape in Jakarta. Over the years, JCC has been the venue of choice for numerous important conferences, exhibitions, fairs, indoor sports events, and musical concerts. Its iconic presence and commitment to excellence have made it a trusted name in the industry.




Facilities and Services

JCC offers a variety of exceptional facilities to meet diverse event needs. Our conference rooms provide the perfect setting for seminars, workshops, and conferences. Our exhibition spaces are designed to showcase products and services effectively. For sports enthusiasts, we provide indoor sports spaces for various activities. Additionally, our musical spaces cater to musicians, bands, and recording enthusiasts. Each of these spaces is equipped with advanced audiovisual capabilities, modern amenities, and flexible seating arrangements to create the perfect ambiance for successful events.




Experienced Staff

At JCC, our experienced and dedicated staff is committed to providing exceptional service and ensuring the seamless execution of events. From event planning and logistics to technical support and on-site management, our team works closely with clients to understand their unique needs and deliver tailored solutions.




ISO 9001:2015 Certification

JCC is proud to hold the ISO 9001:2015 certification, which signifies our commitment to maintaining high-quality standards in all aspects of our operations. This certification recognizes our dedication to providing exceptional services, ensuring customer satisfaction, and continuously improving our processes.




International Association of Congress Centres (AIPC) Certification

JCC is proud to be certified by the International Association of Congress Centres (AIPC). This prestigious certification is awarded to conference centers that demonstrate excellence in facility management, service quality, and sustainability practices. It recognizes JCC's commitment to providing top-notch facilities and services that meet international standards.




LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design)

JCC is proud to have received LEED Certification, recognizing our commitment to sustainable building practices. This prestigious certification acknowledges our efforts to minimize our environmental impact through energy efficiency, water conservation, waste management, and sustainable materials usage. By choosing JCC, our clients can be confident in hosting their events in an environmentally conscious venue that prioritizes sustainability.
